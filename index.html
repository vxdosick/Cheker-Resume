How it works

Welcome to ResumeScanAi. Here you can check the validity of your resume for different types of companies. Get an overall assessment as well as more narrow feedback that will help you improve your resume.

Upload your resume in PDF format and then select the type of company you are most focused on, this will help artificial intelligence better understand where you aspire to go and give a more accurate assessment.

After the review, you will receive an evaluation of your resume, items that positively influenced the evaluation, as well as those that should be changed. And in this you will be helped by general feedback aimed at general statistics and help in correcting your resume.
